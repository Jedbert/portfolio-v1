In Loving Memory of Mr. Idowu Gbemiga Albert (1964 - 2022)

Mr. Idowu Gbemiga Albert, a man known for his passion, deep love, and unwavering care for others, left us peacefully in his sleep on April 30th, 2022. Born in 1964 in Emure, Ekiti State, Nigeria, he lived a life filled with love, devotion, and a commitment to the well-being of his family and community.

A Timeline of a Life Well Lived

  • April 1964: Born in Emure, Ekiti State, Nigeria.
  • December 1995: Married at the age of 31, beginning a beautiful chapter of love and family.
  • June 1996: Welcomed his first child and son, Ebenezer, into the world.
  • January 1999: The family grew with the arrival of their second son, Jedidiah.
  • January 2002: Blessed with a daughter, Victoria, who brought even more joy to their lives.
  • April 2009: His family further expanded with the birth of their daughter, EverFlourish, completing their precious family circle.

Beyond Family: A Man of Faith and Community

Mr. Albert was a devout Christian, his faith guiding his actions and shaping his character. He cherished his roles as a father, friend, uncle, brother, and husband, showering his loved ones with unwavering love and support.

Though he took ill in 2021, his spirit remained strong. He was surrounded by his family's love until he passed away peacefully on April 30th, 2022. A service celebrating his life was held on May 6th, 2022, at the Kuje Christian Cemetery, where he was laid to rest.

A Legacy of Love

Mr. Idowu Gbemiga Albert's absence leaves a void in the hearts of his family and all who knew him. He is remembered for his passion, kindness, and unwavering dedication to those he loved. May his memory be a source of comfort and strength for all who cherish him.
